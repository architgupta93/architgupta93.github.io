Objective

Technology has grown both in size and reach but the world, at large, is still in need for more of it. I think that research should be directed towards improving the quality of life. As of now, I wish to explore a mixture of neuroscience and computer architecture. There is an entire world of possibilities that we can explore by understanding the workings of our own minds, but it seems that nature is miles ahead. Let's see if we can catch up!

Especially in a country like India, where everyone can afford to be educated and scope for developement is enormous, we can make a collective effort to change some of the things for good. Most of the problems that we face are unique because of our demographic structure and history. The best solutions for our problems are therefore indegenous ones!

Research Interests

  • Neuromorphic design

    Neuromorphic architecures incorporating spiking neural networks and plasticity, Mathematical analysis of dynamical systems in neuroscience, Behaviour and electrical modelling of ion channel, Understanding bio-physical phenomenon like music, rhythms and vision

  • Computer architecture

    High performance computing, hardware accelerators in CPU-FPGA setup, GPU design and optimization, application specific SoC design, heterogenous architectures, Control flow analysis
