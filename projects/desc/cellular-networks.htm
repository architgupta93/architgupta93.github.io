 INTRODUCTION

In a cellular network, Base Stations (BS) are set up to meet the peak Quality of Service (QoS) demands, like throughput, Call Drop Ratio (CDR), coverage etc. of the relevant locality. However, the requirements are not the same throughout the day. Operational costs can be drastically cut down by reorganizing the network dynamically. We developed a model to predict the network state (Voice traffic at each of the BSs) by analytically modeling the traffic data of one of Indiaâ€™s leading telecom operators.
